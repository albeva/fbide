FreeBASIC Compiler — Licensing
==============================

FBIde bundles the FreeBASIC compiler (fbc) and its runtime libraries as a
convenience. FreeBASIC is an independent project and is distributed under its
own licences, summarised below.

  * The FreeBASIC compiler itself (fbc and the command-line tools) is released
    under the GNU General Public License (GPL), version 2 or later.

  * The FreeBASIC runtime libraries and the FreeBASIC-specific headers are
    released under the GNU Lesser General Public License (LGPL), version 2.1 or
    later, with the standard exception that programs you compile with FreeBASIC
    may be distributed under any licence of your choosing. In other words,
    programs built with FreeBASIC are yours — you are not required to release
    them under the GPL or LGPL.

  * Some bundled third-party headers and import libraries carry the licence of
    their original upstream project.

The complete licence texts (COPYING.txt / the GPL and LGPL, plus readme.txt and
Contributors.txt) are installed alongside the compiler in the FBIde program
folder. The authoritative source and full documentation are available at:

    https://www.freebasic.net

Installing the FreeBASIC compiler is optional; if you clear the "Install the
FreeBASIC compiler" option on the previous page, only FBIde is installed and
this licence does not apply.
